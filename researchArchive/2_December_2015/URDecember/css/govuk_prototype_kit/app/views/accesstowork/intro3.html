3. What you'll get

How much money you'll get depends on your condition and how much help you need.

The money can pay for things like:

  • adaptations to the equipment you use
  • special equipment
  • fares to work if you can't use public transport
  • a support worker or job coach to help you in your workplace
  • a support service, if you have a mental health condition
  • disability awareness training for your colleagues
  • someone to help you communicate at a job interview
  • the cost of moving your equipment if you change location or job

Changes to Access to Work from 1 October 2015

From 1 October 2015 the maximum Access to Work grant that can be awarded is £40,800.

If your grant was awarded before 1 October 2015 and is worth more than £40,800 it will be reduced to the maximum limit in April 2018.
