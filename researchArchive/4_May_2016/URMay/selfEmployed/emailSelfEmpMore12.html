Carer's Allowance: self-employment details needed

Your application for Carer's allowance has been received.

What you need to do next?

You said you had income from self-employment when you applied for Carer's Allowance.

To complete your application you must now give details of the money that came in and out of your business since 6 April 2014.

You have 2 weeks to send this information. You'll need:

- details of your business costs and payments since 6 April 2014 your business bank account statements may help with this

- the amounts of any pension you've paid into

You can send this information online www.carersallowancesubmitself-employment.co.uk

The costs of working and what you pay into a pension can lower your personal income. Giving accurate information could be the difference between qualifying for Carer's Allowance or not.

You'll usually get a decision on your application within 4 weeks. If you don't give your self-employment details it may take longer.

Make a change to your application

Tell the Carer's Allowance Unit immediately if your circumstances change.

You can't report a change in circumstances by email.

You must report a change in circumstances in one of the following ways:

  1. at GOV.UK or
  2. by contacting the Carer's Allowance Unit at
