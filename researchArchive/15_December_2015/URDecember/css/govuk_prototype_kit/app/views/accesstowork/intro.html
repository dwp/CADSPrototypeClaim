1. Overview

You can apply for an Access to Work grant if you have a disability or health condition that makes it difficult for you to work.

The money is to pay for practical support to help you:

  • start working
  • keep working if you already have a job
  • get to and from work
  • move into self-employment or start a business

The grant is not for business start-up costs.

The money doesn’t have to be paid back and doesn't affect other benefits.
