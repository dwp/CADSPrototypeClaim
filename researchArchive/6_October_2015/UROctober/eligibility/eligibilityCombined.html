Can you get Carer's Allowance?

Do you spend 35 hours or more each week caring for the person you care for?

For example, cooking meals, or helping them with their shopping.

You can only get Carer's Allowance if you care for someone at least 35 hours a week

  • Are you aged 16 or over?

    You can only get Carer's Allowance if you are 16 or over

  • Do you live in England, Scotland or Wales?

    Families of HM Forces should answer "Yes" no matter where they live

    You can normally only get Carer's Allowance if you live in England, Scotland or Wales. You might get it if you live in the EEA (European Economic Area) or Switzerland, but you must apply to find out.

    If you live in Northern Ireland go to NI Carer's Allowance
