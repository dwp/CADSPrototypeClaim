4. Apply

You can apply for an Access to Work grant online.

You'll need:

  • your National Insurance number
  • your workplace address
  • the details of someone at your work who can be contacted if you need a workplace assessment, eg your manager
  • your unique tax reference number, if you’re self-employed
  • the name of your New Enterprise Allowance mentor, if you have one
