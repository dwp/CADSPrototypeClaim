Carer's Allowance - application confirmation

Your claim for Carer's Allowance was received successfully.

What happens next?

You'll usually get a decision on your claim within 4 weeks.

You don't need to do anything else - the Carer's Allowance Unit will contact you if they need more information

Make a change to your application

Tell the Carer's Allowance Unit immediately if your circumstances change.

You can't report a change in circumstances by email.

You must report a change in circumstances in one of the following ways:

  1. at GOV.UK or
  2. by contacting the Carer's Allowance Unit at
