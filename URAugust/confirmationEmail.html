Carer's Allowance - application confirmation

Your claim for Carer's Allowance was received successfully.

What happens next?

You'll usually get a decision on your claim in 4 weeks.

You don't need to do anything - the Carer's Allowance Unit will contact you if they need more information.

Report a change

If your circumstances change, you must report it online at www.gov.uk/carers-allowance-report-change

Alternatively you can report it directly to the Carer's Allowance Unit.
