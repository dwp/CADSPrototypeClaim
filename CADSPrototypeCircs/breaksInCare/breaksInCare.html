Breaks from caring

  • We understand if you don't have exact details for some breaks, include as much information as possible to prevent any unnecessary delays.

  • Do I need to report a break from caring? – Help

    We need to know about any times when, for any reason, you spend less than 35 hours a week caring for the person you care for.
    For example, if they were in respite care, in hospital or on holiday without you, or you were in hospital or on holiday without them.
