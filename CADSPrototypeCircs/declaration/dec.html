Declaration

The declarations below set out your legal responsibilities in respect of your claim.

  • I declare that I understand the Carer's Allowance Claim Notes and that the information provided on this change of circumstances form is correct and complete.
  • I understand that I must report any further changes in my circumstances or those of the person that I am caring for which may affect my entitlement promptly and by failing to do so I may be liable to prosecution or face a financial penalty.
  • I will use the online service or phone 0345 608 4321 or write to Carer's Allowance Unit, Palatine House, Lancaster Road, Preston, PR1 1HB to report a change in my circumstances or that of the person that I am caring for.
  • If I give false or incomplete information or fail to report any future changes in my circumstances or those of the person that I am caring for promptly, I understand that my Carer's Allowance may be stopped or reduced and any overpayment of Carer's Allowance may be recovered.In addition I may be prosecuted or face a financial penalty.
  • Do you agree to us obtaining information from any other persons or organisations you may have told us about?
