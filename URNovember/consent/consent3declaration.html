Declaration

You agree:

  • the information you've given is complete and correct as far as you know
  • you'll pay back any money you've been overpaid if you're asked to
  • you'll report changes to your circumstances promptly

Contacting other people

To complete your application the Industial Injuries Disablement Benefit team may ask the following for more information:

  • your employer
  • people who know about your condition, eg doctors, physiotherapists or counsellors who treated you

 

If you don't agree to this contact, or change your mind about it, then call 0345 758 5433 at any time after sending your application.

You could be prosecuted or have to pay a penalty if you deliberately give wrong or incomplete information, don't report changes or don't repay any money you owe when asked.

The person named as the carer in this application must also agree to these terms before you send your application.
