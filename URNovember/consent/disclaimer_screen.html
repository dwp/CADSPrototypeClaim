What you need to know

    If the person you care for receives certain benefits, the amount they receive may be affected by your application for Carer's Allowance:

    • If they get a Severe Disability Premium with their income-based Jobseekers Allowance, Income Support, income-related Employment and Support Allowance, Housing Benefit, they may no longer get that premium if we pay you Carer's Allowance. Find out more
    • If they get Pension Credit which includes an extra amount for severe disability, they may no longer get that extra amount if we pay you Carer's Allowance. Find out more
    • This could also affect any reduction in Council Tax they may be entitled to. Find out more by contacting your local authority.

    • You should also read the full terms and conditions.

    I declare that:

    • I understand that if I am paid Carer's Allowance it may affect the benefits paid to the person I care for.
    • I will make the person I care for aware that there could be a change to their benefits.
