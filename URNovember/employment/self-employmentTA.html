Employment

Employment
  • Have you been self-employed at any time since a week before your claim date?

    Your claim date is 10/03/2015. Self-employment includes:

    • working for yourself
    • being a partner or sleeping partner in a business
    • getting income from property or land you own
    • renting out any part of the home you live in, eg to a lodger
  • Have you been employed at any time since 6 months before your claim date?

    You claim date is 10/03/2015. Employment includes:

    • full or part-time work, casual or temporary work
    • job sharing
    • being on a career break
    • being a company director
    • being included in any tax return as a worker or a non worker
    • being in the territorial army or other auxillary armed forces
    • receiving a health insurance benefit from your employer

    This doesn't include self-employment
