Employment

Employment
  • Have you been self employed at any time since 10/1/2000?

    This is one week before your claim date. Self employment includes:

    • working for yourself
    • being a partner or sleeping partner in a business
    • receive income from property or land you own
    • renting out any part of the home you live in - i.e. to a lodger
  • Have you been employed at any time since 10/1/2000?

    This is six months before your claim date. Employment includes:

    • full-time or part time work, casual or temporary work
    • job sharing
    • being on a career break
    • being a company director
    • being included in any tax return as a worker or a non worker
    • being in the territorial army or other auxillary armed forces
    • receiving a health insurance benefit from your employer

    This does not include self employment
